HTML

Format :
<!DOCTYPE html> 
<html>
	<head>
		<title>Content</title>
		<meta Charset="UTF-8">
	</head>
	<body>
		Content
	</body>
</html>



Types of html tags:
1) Paired: open+close tag
2) Unpaired:only open tag/self-closing tag

&
1)Inline: action performed on the same line
2)Block:action performed on the new line

Element=completion of a tag

Definition tags:
1)Headings:
<h1></h1>
<h2></h2>
<h3></h3>
<h4></h4>
<h5></h5>
<h6></h6>

2)Paragraph:
<p></p>

3)divisional:
<div></div>

text-formatting tags:
1)Bold:
to bold the content naturally i.e used for large amount of text
<bold>content</bold>

2)strong:
to make content bold schematically
<strong>content</strong>

3)italic:
write down the content in italic font & used for large amount of text
<i>content</i>

4)emphasize:
convert the content to italic 
<em>content</em>

5)insert:
to seem like an inserted content to fill in the blanks
<ins>content</ins>

6)underline:
to underline the content
<u>content</u>

7)mark:
highlight content
<mark>content</mark>
